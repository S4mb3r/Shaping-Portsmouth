The Shaping Portsmouth Charity Support Scheme is a programme designed to link tradesmen who would like to volunteer with our charities and trusts. As a volunteer we are asking you to donate your time and niche skills to help give back to the community. Skills such as programming, building, plumbing and much more will help those charities who require more support in these tough times.

This scheme promises to be an incredibly rewarding and fulfilling experience in which your help would be more than greatly appreciated. Thank you for your support, the link to register is below.


HOW TO HELP

Shaping Portsmouth has a program available to allow tradespeople to offer their expertise in fixing problems for registered charities.
